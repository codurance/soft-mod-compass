You regularly lose team members due to the poor morale caused by the low quality levels as well as to frustration and sheer burnout. This creates a negative feedback loop as the remaining team members struggle to cover increasing gaps in organisational, technical and domain knowledge

First of all you need to identify the areas where legacy code, weak development practices and overall low quality are hurting your organisation the most, so that remediation efforts can be focused where they will bring the most immediate benefit.
